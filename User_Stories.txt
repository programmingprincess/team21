Team #21

User Stories

1.  As a player, I want to make a username so my score can be associated with it.
2.  As a player, I want roll dice so that I can increase my score.
3.  As a player, I want to hold so that I can save my score without risk of losing.
4.  As a player, I want to view my score so that I can know my standing compared to other players.
5.  As a player, I want to add more players so that I may compete with my friends.
6.  As a player, I want to compete with an AI so I can play alone.
